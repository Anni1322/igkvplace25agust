How to fill the application form?

  • Click on Online Counseling Apply / Edit from left side panel to apply.
  • Form is divided into parts. Fill all of them properly.
  • In Basic Details, fill your personal details and address details.
  • In Academic Details, fill your last academic details and upload your recent photo and signature.
  • In Preference Details, set your college/subject preference carefully.
  • In Document Details, upload scanned file of required documents. Some of them are mandatory to upload, and upload others if they are applicable.
  • Preview & Declaration shows the filled details. Verify if all the information is correct. Correction can be made if required by clicking on Go to Edit button.

Photo & Signature files

  • Photo should be less than 50KB in size.
  • Signature should be less than 20KB in size.
  • Photo and signature should be JPG/JPEG file only.

Document files

  • Scanned copy of document should be 250KB-500KB in size.
  • File should be JPG/JPEG/PDF file only.
  • Uploaded document should be very clear to read.
